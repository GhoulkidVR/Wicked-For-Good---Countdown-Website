About Wicked, For Good

Mark your calendars for November 21, 2025, when "Wicked, For Good" will be released at midnight (local time). This highly anticipated new chapter in the Wicked saga promises to delve deeper into the complex relationship between Elphaba and Glinda, exploring themes of friendship, prejudice, and the nature of good and evil.

The original musical "Wicked" reimagined the Land of Oz, telling the story of two young women - one born with emerald-green skin who is smart, fiery and misunderstood, the other blonde, ambitious and exceptionally popular. How these two unlikely friends end up as the Wicked Witch of the West and Glinda the Good Witch makes for the most complete and satisfying musical in decades.

For more information about the release, visit Wicked's official website.
